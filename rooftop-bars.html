Rooftop Bar Booking Tips

Book in Advance

Popular rooftop bars fill up quickly, especially on weekends. Make reservations 1-2 weeks ahead for the best tables and times.

Optimal Timing

Arrive for sunset (golden hour) for the best views and photos. Happy hour typically runs 4-7 PM with better prices.

Dress Code

Most upscale rooftops enforce smart casual to formal dress codes. Check requirements when booking to avoid disappointment.

Weather Backup

Many rooftops close during bad weather. Have indoor backup plans and check cancellation policies before booking.
