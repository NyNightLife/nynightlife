What Makes Brooklyn Special

Unique nightlife experiences you can only find in Brooklyn

Craft Beer Capital

Brooklyn is home to some of NYC's best breweries and craft beer bars, from famous Brooklyn Brewery to hidden taprooms.

  • Brooklyn Brewery - The OG Brooklyn beer
  • Other Half Brewing - Hazy IPAs
  • Threes Brewing - Gowanus taproom
  • Evil Twin Brewing - Experimental brews
Brewery Tours

Live Music Venues

From intimate indie rock clubs to electronic music warehouses, Brooklyn's music scene is unmatched in diversity and quality.

  • Music Hall of Williamsburg - Indie & alternative
  • Baby's All Right - Emerging artists
  • Elsewhere - Electronic & experimental
  • Kings Theatre - Historic concert hall
Concert Tickets

Skyline Rooftops

Brooklyn's rooftops offer the best views of Manhattan's skyline, creating magical backdrops for drinks and dining experiences.

  • Westlight - 22nd floor panoramic views
  • 1 Hotel Rooftop - Brooklyn Bridge views
  • Berry Park - Massive rooftop space
  • Harriet's Rooftop - 1 Hotels skyline bar
